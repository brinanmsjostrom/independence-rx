About

Independence RX began initially as a project by me, Brinan Sjostrom, to gather and share knowledge about self-reliance, freedom, and the dangers of big technology. Over time, my hope is that it will grow into a community effort.

I believe that every step toward self-reliance is a step toward freedom. The system we live under are designed to create dependency and offering a solution to problems it created in the first place. By reclaiming the ability to cook, build, grow, repair, and prepare, we reduce that dependency and expand our freedom.

This site is meant to be a resource: practical, open, and shared freely. I hope you find something here that empowers you to live more independently.

Together, we can build a library of knowledge that strengthens individual freedom.
